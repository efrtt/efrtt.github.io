Process

The typical commission will go through like this:

  • I receive a commission.
  • I do the outline of the painting, and send it to the customer for approval by email.
  • After being approved, (howevermany times it takes) I will begin the rest of the process. Note: I will not finish the rest of the painting until I get an approval.
