The process that will usually be taken with commissions:
  1. A commission is sent
  2. Unless my schedule doesn't allow it, I will usually start the commission the day of/day after receiving it.
  3. A rough sketch of the image will be sent to the email of the commissioner.
  4. After having the sketch approved, I will send an invoice. Note: I will NOT finish the rest of the commission until the sketch is approved.
  5. The commissioner pays the invoice, and I finish the commission.
  6. The commission will be sent via email as a high resolution .png, unless a different format is otherwise stated in the Additional Notes part of the commission form.
